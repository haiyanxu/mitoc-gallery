Imagestore. Django image gallery

Powerful image management interface
and gallery for your users.

You can download imagestore on github: github.com/hovel/imagestore or install from pypi.

Imagestore gives you

  • Albums
  • Mass upload
  • Thumbnails in admin intereface
  • Ordering
  • Djnago-tagging support
  • Easy PrettyPhoto integration

Gallery for your users

You can use imagestore to create gallery for your users.
  • Users can create albums, upload photos to albums
  • Users can make albums non-public
  • Users can set name, descripion and tags for photos
  • Users can edit infomation about photo or upload new veresion
